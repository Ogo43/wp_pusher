FAQs

Who are mobile employee?

A mobile employee typically refers to a worker who is not tied to a desk and often needs to work while on the move. Mobile employees are common in roles that require fieldwork, sales, retail, delivery services, or any job where mobility is essential to the work process.

What is micro training

Micro training is a modern approach to learning and development that involves delivering small, focused, and bite-sized training modules or lessons to learners.

These modules are designed to be easily consumable, typically lasting only a few minutes. Micro training enhances learning retention, and allows for flexible, on-the-go learning.
